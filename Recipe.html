Southern Red Velvet Cake

Red Velet Cake is a southern staple around the holidays! You do not want to miss out on this rich delecay. This recipe will be sure to bring you back to warm memories of the holidays and fill your soul will joy.

Ingredients

Crisco Vegitable Shortening Oil for Pans
2 1/2 cups of flour
1/2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon fine salt
1 tablespoon cocoa powder
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
1 cup of buttermilk- room temp
2 large eggs- room temp
2 tablespoons red food coloring(1 ounce)
1 teaspoon white vinegar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Cream Cheese frosting icing recipe follows
Crushed Pecans to decorate

Cream Cheesse Frosting

    1 pound cream cheese
    4 cups sifted confectioners sugar
    2 sticks unsalted butter(1 cup) sofened
    1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Cooking Instructuions


    1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees and lightly grease and flour three by 9 1/2 inch round cake pans.
    2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, salt, sugar, and cocao powder. In another larger bowl, whisk together the oil, buttermil, eggs, food, coloring, vinegar, and vanilla.
    3. Mix in dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined and a smooth batter is formed. DO NOT OVER MIX BATTER.
    4. Divide the batter evenlly among the cake pans. Tap pans slightly to remove any air pockets inside the batter that may have formed when pouring.
    5. Place pans inside of the oven evenly spaced apart. Bake the pans, while rotating them slightly have way through the coooking time around 30mins.
    6. Check cakes, with a toothpick and if it comes out clean they are done baking.
    7. Remove the cakes and run a butter knife around the sides, turn the cakes upside down onto plates to remove from pans.
    8. LET THE CAKES COOL COMPLETLEY BEFORE ICING.
    9. Frost cakes evenly with icing between each layer. You may use a spatula or palette knife for this. Spread crushed pecans around the sides and top of the cake as desired and enjoy!

    Cream Cheese Frosting Instructions


    1. In a bowl, with a standing or handheld mixer, mix the cream chesse, sugar, and butter on low speed until evenly mixed.
    2. Increase spped until mix is light and fluffy, around 5 mins.
    3. Reduce speed of mixed to low and add vanilla, mix again until evenly mixed.
    4. Store in fridge until cakes have cooled to frost.
